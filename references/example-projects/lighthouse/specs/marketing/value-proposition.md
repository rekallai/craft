# Value Proposition — Lighthouse

## Headline

**Get paid without chasing.**

## Sub-headline

Lighthouse sends your invoices, follows up on late payments, and reconciles deposits — so you can spend Friday afternoons doing something other than dunning.

## The three lines that earn the click

1. **Invoices that send themselves.** Set the schedule once. Lighthouse handles the rest — including reminders before, on, and after the due date.
2. **Payments that reconcile themselves.** When the deposit hits your bank, we match it to the right invoice. You see one number per month, not a thousand line items.
3. **Hours back, every month.** The dashboard counts the hours Lighthouse saved you. Most users see 8–14 hours/month after the first 30 days.

## Who it's for

- Small-business owners (1–10 people) who invoice repeat or one-off clients
- Service businesses: agencies, consultants, contractors, freelancers
- People for whom QuickBooks is "too much" and a Word-doc invoice is "too little"

## Who it isn't for (yet)

- Businesses with complex tax situations (multi-state, multi-currency, sales tax)
- Companies that need a full GL / chart of accounts
- Enterprises looking for procurement integrations or custom workflows

## Proof we'll lead with

- Time-saved counter (real number, surfaced in-product and in marketing)
- "Sent your first invoice in under 10 minutes" guarantee
- ACH + card with sub-2% effective fees on most plans

## What we don't say

- "All-in-one." We're not all-in-one. That's the point.
- "Replace your accountant." We don't. We make their job easier.
- "Powered by AI." It is. We don't lead with it.
